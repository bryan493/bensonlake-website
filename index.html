The Lake

A hidden gem of the Pacific Northwest

Benson Lake is one of Mason County's best-kept secrets — a tranquil, non-motorized lake surrounded by towering evergreens. No jet skis. No speedboats. Just paddleboards gliding across glass-still water and eagles circling overhead.

The lake is limited to electric motors only, preserving the pristine quiet that makes this place special. Kayak at sunrise. Fish from the dock. Swim off the shore. Or simply sit with your coffee and watch the otters play.

Just 1.5 hours from Seattle and 30 minutes from Olympia — close enough for a long weekend, remote enough to feel worlds away.
